> For Colosseum hackathon video submission. Record with Loom.
> Practice until you can deliver this naturally in under 3 minutes.

---

## Structure (follows Colosseum's recommended format)

### 1. Hook + Team Background (30 seconds)

> "Every year, investors pour $300 billion into startups. And every year, they get burned by unverifiable metrics. FTX claimed $16 billion in revenue. Theranos claimed $100 million. Nobody could independently verify those numbers — until now."
>
> "I'm [Name], and we're building ChainTrust — the trust layer for startup fundraising on Solana."
>
> "Our team combines [X years] in crypto development with [X years] in startup finance. We've seen the verification problem from both sides — as founders trying to prove our metrics, and as investors trying to verify them."

---

### 2. The Problem (30 seconds)

> "Right now, if an investor wants to verify a startup's MRR, they have two options: trust the founder's word, or pay a Big 4 firm $50,000 for an audit that takes 6 weeks."
>
> "Neither option works for early-stage investing. The result? Investors make decisions based on unverified claims. Startups with real traction get lost in a sea of inflated metrics. And trust — the most important currency in fundraising — has no infrastructure."

---

### 3. The Solution + Why Crypto (30 seconds)

> "ChainTrust solves this with three cryptographic primitives that can only exist on-chain:"
>
> "First, startups publish their metrics through our platform. We SHA-256 hash every data point and store the proof on Solana. It costs $0.00025 per verification — that's 200,000x cheaper than a traditional audit."
>
> "Second, Pyth oracles independently verify the data against real-world sources. No human intermediary."
>
> "Third, verified startups receive a soulbound NFT certificate — a compressed NFT that lives permanently in their wallet. Any investor, anywhere in the world, can verify it instantly for free."

---

### 4. Market Opportunity (20 seconds)

> "The RegTech market is $4.9 billion and growing 23% per year. 850,000 startups raise funds globally every year. And with the EU Digital Product Passport mandate rolling out, regulatory verification is becoming mandatory, not optional."
>
> "We're starting with Solana ecosystem startups — over 1,000 projects that already live on-chain — and expanding to traditional startups entering crypto through tokenization."

---

### 5. Live Demo (40 seconds)

> "Let me show you how it works on devnet."
>
> [Screen share — show the demo flow]
>
> "Here's a startup registering on ChainTrust. They enter their name, category, and connect their Solana wallet. One transaction — they're on-chain."
>
> "Now they publish their monthly metrics: $142K MRR, 12,847 users, 23% growth rate. Watch — our system computes a SHA-256 proof hash and writes it to Solana."
>
> "The oracle network verifies each metric independently. Revenue matches Stripe data. User count matches analytics. Growth rate confirmed. Trust score: 94 out of 100."
>
> "And here's the soulbound badge landing in their wallet — a compressed NFT certificate proving they're verified. Non-transferable, permanent, and independently verifiable by anyone."
>
> [Show the investor side]
>
> "Now an investor can screen all verified startups, run AI-powered due diligence, compare metrics side-by-side, and track their portfolio — all backed by on-chain proof."

---

### 6. Traction + Business Model (20 seconds)

> "We've onboarded [X] beta startups on devnet who've completed the full verification cycle. Early feedback: [quote from beta tester]."
>
> "Our business model is freemium SaaS. Basic verification is free — funded by Solana's $0.00025 transaction costs. Revenue comes from Pro tier subscriptions at $[X]/month for institutional features: AI due diligence, LP reports, API access, and advanced screening."
>
> "CMT token staking creates aligned incentives: investors stake to access premium data, startups benefit from more engaged investors."

---

### 7. Close (10 seconds)

> "ChainTrust turns startup metrics from trust-me-bro claims into cryptographic proof. We're building this full-time, and Solana is the only chain where this is economically viable at scale."
>
> "We're ChainTrust. Thank you."

---

## Presentation Tips

1. **Record with Loom** — screen + face cam. Judges want to see you.
2. **Show the live demo** — don't just talk about it. Switch to the browser and click through.
3. **Stay under 3 minutes** — judges review hundreds of submissions. Respect their time.
4. **Speak naturally** — don't read a script. Know the key points and flow between them.
5. **End with confidence** — "we're building this full-time" is the most important signal for Colosseum.
